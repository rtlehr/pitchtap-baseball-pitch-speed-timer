PitchTap – Baseball Pitch Speed Timer

PitchTap allows coaches, players, and parents to measure baseball pitch speed using a simple two-tap timer.

How it Works

1. Tap when the pitcher releases the ball.
2. Tap again when the catcher catches it.
3. PitchTap calculates the pitch speed in MPH instantly.

Features

  • Two-tap pitch speed measurement
  • Track fastballs, curves, sliders, and misc pitches
  • Session averages and high/low speeds
  • Pitch history tracking
  • Email pitch reports
  • Adjustable pitching distances
